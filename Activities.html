Legal Aid

Through our partners and networks such as Kituo cha Sheria and Kenya Human Rights Commission (KHRC), SALAR volunteers it's members to offer free legal aid services. We are currently working on partnerships with community justice centres to provide manpower for the establishment of consistent legal aid sessions for those who cannot afford to pay for legal advice within communities.

Legal Research

Promoting the progression of meaningful legal discourse amongst young legal scholars is at the core of our mission as a club. We have established the SALAR Law Journal that provides a platform for students to publish their legal opinions on various legal issues.
