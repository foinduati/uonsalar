Few Words About Us

The Students’ Association for Legal Aid and Research (SALAR) is a non-profit making students’ organization established at the University of Nairobi, School of Law in 1999.

Objectives of SALAR

  • To promote and undertake legal research as a key component of legal education;
  • To provide free legal aid to persons who cannot afford to pay for the services of a lawyer;
  • To provide community outreach services;
  • To cultivate a public interest lawyering spirit in law students; and
  • To network with NGOs, lawyers, and public and private sector institutions with the aim of expanding the scope of social justice.
